Meet Our Team SUAS 2025

Who Are We?

Skythunder Team was founded in 2016 to help with satisfying the curiosity of conquering what lies above in the sky and help with fast delivery operations and scout endangered situations that are too hard to reach.

Our Aim

At the heart of our UAV manufacturing team lies a relentless drive to innovate, educate, and compete. Each year, we design and build our UAS entirely from scratch, pushing our technical limits to meet the evolving challenges of international competitions like SUAS (Organised by Robonation) and DBF (Organised by AIAA), local contests such as ICMTC (Organised by MTC). For us, UAS development isn't just a project, it's a passion and a vast field to improve our skills, creativity, and teamwork. We aim not only to excel engineering challenges but also to inspire and mentor new team members, ensuring that knowledge, passion, and excellence are passed forward with every generation.

MTC

The Military Technical College (MTC) in Egypt is not just a place for study. It's the foundation of our identity as engineers and innovators. As proud students of MTC we represent our institution in every UAV competition we enter, carrying forward its legacy of military discipline and academic excellence. MTC is our sole sponsor and greatest supporter that generously provide us with resources, knowledge, and values that grow our passion. Every challenge that we face is an opportunity to become more than we've been before. as we are the reflection of our college's commitment to develop our technical & leadership skills as the leaders of the future.
